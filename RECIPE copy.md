# Peace Offering Cake Recipes

## 1. Classic Vanilla Cake
- 2 ½ cups flour, 1 ½ cups sugar, 1 cup butter, 1 cup milk, 4 eggs, 1 tbsp baking powder, 1 tsp vanilla, ½ tsp salt.
- Bake at 350°F for 30-35 mins.

## 2. Rich Chocolate Cake
- 2 cups sugar, 1 ¾ cups flour, ¾ cup cocoa, 1 ½ tsp baking powder, 1 ½ tsp baking soda, 1 tsp salt, 2 eggs, 1 cup milk, ½ cup oil, 2 tsp vanilla, 1 cup boiling water.
- Bake at 350°F for 30-35 mins.

## 3. Red Velvet Cake
- 2 ½ cups flour, 1 ½ cups sugar, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp cocoa powder, 1 cup buttermilk, 2 eggs, 1 ½ cups oil, 1 tsp vinegar, red food coloring.
- Bake at 350°F for 30 mins.

## 4. Lemon Drizzle Cake
- 225g butter, 225g sugar, 225g flour, 4 eggs, 1 lemon (zest), baking powder. Glaze: 175g sugar, 1 lemon (juice).
- Bake at 350°F for 45-50 mins.

## 5. Carrot Cake
- 2 cups flour, 2 cups sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp cinnamon, 3 cups grated carrots, 1 cup oil, 4 eggs.
- Bake at 350°F for 40-50 mins.

## 6. Coffee Cake
- 2 cups flour, 1 cup brown sugar, ¾ cup butter, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1 cup sour cream, 1 egg, 1 tsp baking soda.
- Bake at 350°F for 35 mins.

## 7. Cheesecake
- 24 oz cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, 3 eggs, 1 cup sour cream, 1 tbsp vanilla, graham cracker crust.
- Bake at 325°F for 55 mins in a water bath.

## 8. Sponge Cake
- 1 cup flour, 1 cup sugar, 4 eggs, 1 tsp baking powder, 2 tbsp melted butter.
- Bake at 350°F for 25-30 mins.

## 9. Banana Bread (Cake-style)
- 3 ripe bananas, 1/3 cup melted butter, 1 cup sugar, 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla, 1 tsp baking soda, 1.5 cups flour.
- Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.

## 10. Pound Cake
- 1 lb butter, 1 lb sugar, 1 lb eggs (approx 9), 1 lb flour, 2 tsp vanilla.
- Bake at 325°F for 60-75 mins.
